Some Warren Buffett nuggets

  • The chains of habit are too light to be felt until they are too heavy to be broken
  • Someone is sitting in the shade today because somebody planted a tree a long time ago
  • Study not only what to do, but what not to do
  • Never ask a barber if you need a haircut
  • What you don't do is just as important as what you do
  • Questions compel us to think, Answers tell us whether to act
  • Price is what you pay, Value is what you get
  • It takes 20 years to build a reputation and five minutes to ruin it, If you think about that, you'll do things differently
  • The best thing I did was to choose the right heroes
